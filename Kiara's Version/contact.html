About The StudySpots application works to streamline the process of finding your new favorite place to study on Case Western Reserve’s campus! StudySpots provides a comprehensive analysis of accessibility, availability, noise level, dining options, and everything associated with the best study spots at CWRU! This site also provides a way to reserve a spot, and view the availability schedules for each individual spot! CWRU Campus Planning
Study Spot Features
Food Availability Vending/Cafe

This icon will let you know what food is available. For example, if there are vending machines, restaurants, or cafes.

Outlet Availability Charge devices

This icon will let you know if there are outlets to charge your devices

Noise Level Noisy

This icon will tell you if this study spot is typically more loud or quiet to fit your study needs.

Major

This icon will let you know what the most common major is that may study here. "All" means all majors are welcome while "Math" means that it is likely several math classes are held in this building indicating that you'll most likely encounter a student majoring in math.

Restrictions N/A

This icon can mean a few things: students may not have access to this building, this building is undergoing renovations, or other Restrictions.
